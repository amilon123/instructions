Product Placement

  • Dos
  • Don'ts

Approved locations

  • Indoors
  • Open areas
  • Firm, flat, level surfaces
  • Away from heat sources
  • Cool areas with heat levels less than 50%
  • Clear of heavy traffic areas
  • 4m (10cm) away from walls with moisture outlet facing away from the wall

Unapproved locations

  • Outdoors
  • Closed Rooms
  • Overhanging or tilted surfaces
  • Directly next to or on top of heat registers
  • Humid areas with heat levels in excess of 50%
  • In the way of heavy traffic areas
  • Directly next to the wall with moisture outlet directly facing the wall
